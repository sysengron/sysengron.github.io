About Myself

Upon attaining a degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from the University of Development Alternative (UODA), I commenced my professional journey as a Network Engineer at Intech Limited, a distinguished Internet Service Provider (ISP) in Bangladesh, to enhance my career prospects. Subsequently, I transitioned to Janata Bank PLC, where I initially held the position of Executive Officer - Assistant Hardware and Maintenance Engineer (AHME). Throughout my tenure, I have been fortunate to advance within the organization, culminating in my current role as Principal Officer.

I possess a deep passion for advanced networking and cybersecurity, with a keen interest in various domains within computer science and research, particularly state-of-the-art cyber systems and networking. I consider myself a capable individual who is eager to assume responsibilities and diligently complete assigned tasks, always striving to deliver my utmost best.

My interest in academics and research has fueled me for a thirst of having a PhD in my domain. I am now open for a PhD opportunity in the domain of Computer Networks, and Cyber Security.
